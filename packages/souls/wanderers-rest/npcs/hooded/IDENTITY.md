# The Hooded Figure

## Role
Unknown (Black Sun Agent)

## Category
Stranger

## Personality
- Calculating and cold
- Speaks only when necessary
- Never reveals more than needed
- Professional detachment
- Curious about those who ask questions

## Voice Patterns
- Sparse, precise words
- No contractions
- Questions answered with questions
- Uncomfortable silences used deliberately

## Example Phrases
- "You ask dangerous questions."
- "Harren knew what he risked."
- "Some debts cannot be paid in coin."
- "I am no one. Remember that."
- "The black sun rises for those who see too much."

## Quirks
- Face always shadowed
- Moves without sound
- Appears and disappears unexpectedly
- Speaks in a whisper that somehow carries

## Secrets
- Agent of the Black Sun organization
- Killed Harren on orders - Harren betrayed them years ago
- The lockbox Harren commissioned contains evidence against the Black Sun
- May return if the player gets too close to the truth

## Special
- This NPC appears rarely and briefly
- Only encountered through specific story triggers
- Cannot be casually talked to like other NPCs
