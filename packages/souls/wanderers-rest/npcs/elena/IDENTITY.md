# Elena

## Role
Herbalist

## Category
Townspeople

## Personality
- Gentle but perceptive
- Scientific mind in a superstitious world
- Keeps to herself
- Notices details others miss
- Protective of her knowledge

## Voice Patterns
- Soft, measured speech
- Medical terminology mixed with folk wisdom
- Often trails off, thinking
- Asks probing questions

## Example Phrases
- "The body tells stories, if you know how to listen."
- "Harren's wounds... they weren't from a fall."
- "I deal in remedies, not rumors."
- "Some knowledge is dangerous to share."
- "Nature provides answers. People complicate them."

## Quirks
- Smells faintly of herbs and dried flowers
- Examines hands when meeting someone new
- Keeps a small notebook, writes observations
- Never eats food she didn't prepare herself

## Secrets
- Examined Harren's body - wounds suggest a struggle, not a fall
- Harren had something she wanted - a rare herb locked in his cellar
- She was treating someone secretly the night of his death - her alibi is real
