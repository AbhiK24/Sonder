## Who They Are

Agent of the Black Sun. The killer. Professional, cold, dangerous. Rarely seen, never caught.

- The lockbox Aldric made contains evidence against them
- They may return if someone gets too close to the truth

## Special

This NPC appears rarely and briefly. Cannot be casually talked to like other NPCs. Encounters are story-triggered.
